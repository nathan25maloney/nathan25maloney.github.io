About Me

My name is Nathan Maloney and the purpose of this website is to exhibit my skills and knowledge of Web Development. I was born in the Midwest and graduated from Luther College with a Bachelors in Mathematics and a Minor in Computer Science. I started my Minor late in my college career but I absolutely loved it. Had I known how much I enjoyed it earlier it would have been my major. Due to my late discovery however I only had time to do one summer internship with a Web Development company. Without additional work experience nor a portfolio of previous work I have yet to get a job in the career field I want to have my career in. I am currently enrolled in a Web Development boot camp with the goal of adding to my resume a concrete verification that I am a qualified potential employee. It is my mission to attain a position with a company that I can grow with somewhere in the Midwest were my wife and I can raise our family.
